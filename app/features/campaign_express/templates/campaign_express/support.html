Frequently Asked Questions

To create a campaign, click on New Campaign from the dashboard. Set a campaign name, choose your communication channel (Voice call or WhatsApp templates), write the dynamic script using placeholder tags like {{"{?"}}name{{"?}"}}, and upload a CSV file containing your list of contact numbers.

Payment is only required when executing/launching campaigns. Creating, duplicating, and configuring drafts is completely free of charge. You pay a calculated amount based on the number of targets in your upload contact list (currently priced at ₹1.50 per contact).

To ensure platform transmission quality, prevent spam calls, and comply with telecom guidelines, Campaign Express requires all standalone users to complete a basic profile identity verification before executing live campaigns. You can complete verification in the Profile section by uploading a scanned government ID.

Our platform administrators review and approve identity verification requests manually. The process usually takes between 24 and 48 hours. You will receive an alert on your dashboard as soon as verification completes.

Reports are auto-generated as soon as a running campaign completes. You can find them under the Reports tab in the sidebar navigation. Each report contains aggregate statistics (connection rate, average call duration) and detailed delivery logs, which you can export in CSV format.
