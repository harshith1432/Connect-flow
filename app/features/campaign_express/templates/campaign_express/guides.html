Standard Campaign Express Lifecycle

1
Register Individual Profile

Sign up instantly using Google Auth or manual email registration. Access the Campaign Express panel immediately.

2
Create Campaign Draft

Click "New Campaign". Choose voice calling or WhatsApp text channels to configure your objectives.

3
Upload Contact spreadsheet

Drag-and-drop a CSV spreadsheet containing recipient details. Make sure to define a phone number column clearly.

4
Configure Script Placeholders

Draft the text content of your script. Link columns in your CSV using dynamic tags like {{"{{"}}name{{"}}"}}.

5
Submit Identity verification

To comply with telecom standards, upload government documents (Aadhaar, PAN, Voter ID) to unlock campaign runs.

6
Pay & Launch Run

Initiate checkout. Process the calculated charge securely via Razorpay. Your campaign begins immediately on success.

7
Receive Reports & Analytics

Review delivery success rates and call summaries. Download transaction invoices and export CSV reports.
