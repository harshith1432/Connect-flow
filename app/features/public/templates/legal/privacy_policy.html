1

Information We Collect

1.1 Account Registration Data

When you create an organisation account on CalltoConvey, we collect:

  • Organisation name, type, industry, country, and registered address
  • Administrator full name, email address, phone number, and designation
  • Support contact details (email and phone)
  • Optional: Organisation logo image

1.2 Communication Data

In the course of using our platform to run outbound campaigns, we collect and process:

  • Audience records (names, phone numbers, and any custom fields you define)
  • Message scripts and audio content you create
  • Delivery logs (call status, WhatsApp delivery receipts, timestamps)
  • Twilio / Hooman Labs API credentials you optionally provide (stored encrypted)

1.3 Usage Data

We automatically collect technical usage data including IP addresses, browser type, session identifiers, login timestamps, and pages visited. This data is used solely for security monitoring and platform improvement.

Note: We never sell your personal data or audience contact lists to any third party, ever.

2

How We Use Your Data

We use the information collected to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the CalltoConvey platform
  • Process and dispatch your outbound voice and WhatsApp campaigns
  • Verify your organisation's identity during the approval process
  • Send platform notifications, support responses, and billing alerts
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, brute-force attacks, and security threats
  • Comply with applicable laws and regulatory requirements
  • Improve our TTS engine and campaign delivery accuracy using anonymised aggregate statistics

We will never use your audience contact data or campaign content for any purpose beyond delivering your campaigns.

3

Data Sharing

We only share your data with third parties in the following limited circumstances:

  • Twilio Inc. — To deliver voice calls and WhatsApp messages on your behalf. Twilio processes phone numbers and call content under their own Privacy Policy.
  • Hooman Labs — Optional alternative communication provider. Used only if you configure it.
  • Google (TTS API) — Text converted to speech is processed by Google's Text-to-Speech API. No personal data beyond the script text is transmitted.
  • Legal requirements — If required by a court order, government authority, or to protect the rights and safety of CalltoConvey, its users, or the public.

Important: CalltoConvey uses strict multi-tenant data isolation. Your organisation's data is never accessible by any other tenant on the platform.

4

Data Retention

We retain your data for the following periods:

  • Account data: Retained for the duration of your subscription plus 90 days after cancellation
  • Campaign & delivery logs: Retained for 12 months from campaign creation date
  • Module records (audience data): Retained until you delete them or close your account
  • Security audit logs: Retained for 6 months
  • Billing data: Retained for 7 years as required by Indian GST regulations

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

5

Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data for certain purposes
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at {{ platform_branding.privacy_email }}. We will respond within 30 days.

6

Cookies

CalltoConvey uses only essential, functional cookies. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

  • Session cookie: Keeps you logged in securely during your session
  • CSRF token cookie: Protects against cross-site request forgery attacks
  • Theme preference: Stores your dark/light mode preference locally

No third-party analytics or advertising cookies are used on any part of the platform.

7

Security

CalltoConvey implements enterprise-grade security measures to protect your data:

  • AES-256 encryption for all stored API credentials
  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • Multi-Factor Authentication (TOTP) for all user roles
  • Brute-force protection with automatic account lockout
  • Session fingerprinting and anomaly detection
  • Content Security Policy headers against XSS attacks
  • Strict multi-tenant data isolation at the database query level

While we implement best-practice security controls, no system can guarantee 100% security. We will notify you promptly in the event of any data breach that affects your account.

8

Third-Party Services

Links to external services within CalltoConvey (such as Twilio dashboards, payment gateways, or API documentation) are governed by those services' own privacy policies. We are not responsible for the data practices of third-party services.

9

Children's Privacy

CalltoConvey is a B2B enterprise platform intended for use by organisations and their authorised administrators. It is not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has submitted data on our platform, contact us immediately.

10

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via email or an in-app platform notification at least 14 days before the changes take effect. Your continued use of CalltoConvey after notification constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
