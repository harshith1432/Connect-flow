Data Processing Agreement

This DPA governs how CalltoConvey processes personal data on behalf of your organisation, in accordance with applicable data protection laws including India's PDPB and the EU GDPR.

This DPA is incorporated into and forms part of the CalltoConvey Terms of Service. By using the platform, your organisation (as Data Controller) agrees to these data processing terms, and CalltoConvey (as Data Processor) agrees to process data only as described herein.

1

Definitions

  • "Data Controller" — the Organisation that determines the purposes and means of processing Personal Data (i.e., you, our customer)
  • "Data Processor" — CalltoConvey, which processes Personal Data on behalf of the Data Controller
  • "Personal Data" — any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person (e.g., names and phone numbers of your audience contacts)
  • "Processing" — any operation performed on Personal Data (collection, storage, transmission, deletion)
  • "Data Subject" — the individuals whose Personal Data is being processed (your audience contacts)
  • "Sub-Processor" — a third-party engaged by CalltoConvey to assist in processing (e.g., Twilio, Google TTS)
2

Roles & Responsibilities

For the purposes of this DPA:

  • Your organisation acts as the Data Controller — you determine what contact data is uploaded, for what purpose, and to whom messages are sent
  • CalltoConvey acts as the Data Processor — we process that data solely on your documented instructions to deliver campaigns
  • CalltoConvey will not process your data for any purpose other than providing the contracted services
3

Categories of Data We Process

Category Data Types Source
Audience Contact Data Names, phone numbers, custom module fields Uploaded by your organisation
Account Data Admin names, emails, phone numbers, designation Provided during registration
Communication Data Call recordings (not stored), delivery logs, message status Generated by campaigns
Worker Data Worker names, emails, roles, campaign assignments Created by Organisation Admin
Technical Data IP addresses, session tokens, login timestamps Automatically collected
4

Purpose of Processing

CalltoConvey processes Personal Data exclusively for the following purposes, as instructed by you:

  • Delivering automated voice call campaigns to your audience contacts
  • Sending WhatsApp messages to your audience contacts via approved providers
  • Storing and managing your campaign data and delivery logs
  • Providing analytics and reports on campaign performance
  • Maintaining platform security and fraud prevention
  • Billing and subscription management
5

We will notify you of any intended changes to sub-processors at least 14 days in advance.

6

Technical & Organisational Security Measures

CalltoConvey implements and maintains the following security measures:

  • Encryption at rest: AES-256 for stored credentials and sensitive fields
  • Encryption in transit: TLS 1.2+ for all data transmission
  • Access control: Role-based access control (RBAC) with principle of least privilege
  • Multi-tenant isolation: All database queries are scoped to the requesting organisation
  • Authentication: MFA enforcement, brute-force lockout, and session fingerprinting
  • Audit logging: Comprehensive audit trail of all administrative actions
  • Vulnerability management: Regular dependency updates and security patch deployment
7

Data Subject Rights Assistance

When you receive a request from a Data Subject (one of your audience contacts) exercising their rights (access, deletion, correction, etc.), CalltoConvey will:

  • Provide reasonable technical assistance to help you fulfil the request within your platform data
  • Not independently respond to Data Subject requests on your behalf
  • Notify you if we receive a Data Subject request addressed directly to us

You remain responsible for responding to your own Data Subjects within the legally required timelines.

8

Data Breach Notification

In the event of a confirmed personal data breach affecting your organisation's data, CalltoConvey will:

  • Notify you by email within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach
  • Provide a description of the nature of the breach, categories affected, and likely consequences
  • Describe the measures taken or proposed to address the breach

You are responsible for notifying your own Data Subjects and relevant supervisory authorities as required by law.

Incident reporting: Security incidents must be reported immediately to: {{ platform_branding.privacy_email }}

9

International Data Transfers

Some of our sub-processors (Twilio, Google, Microsoft) are based in the USA. Where personal data is transferred outside India or the EEA, CalltoConvey ensures appropriate safeguards are in place, including Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and adequacy decisions where applicable.

10

Data Retention & Deletion

Upon termination of your subscription:

  • Your module records and campaign data are retained for a 90-day recovery period
  • After 90 days, all data is permanently and irreversibly deleted from our systems
  • Delivery logs and audit trails are deleted after 12 months from creation
  • Billing records are retained for 7 years as required by Indian GST regulations

You may request earlier deletion of any specific dataset by contacting {{ platform_branding.privacy_email }}.

11

Audit Rights

Enterprise plan customers may request a written summary of CalltoConvey's security and data processing practices once per calendar year. We will respond within 30 business days. Physical or technical audits of CalltoConvey's infrastructure are not available for security reasons but may be replaced with certified third-party audit reports upon request.

12

Governing Law

This DPA is governed by the laws of India, including the Information Technology Act 2000 and applicable data protection regulations. For customers in the EU/EEA, GDPR provisions shall apply to the extent relevant.
