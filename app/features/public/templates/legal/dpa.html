Data Processing Agreement (DPA)

Last Updated: January 24, 2026

Important: This Data Processing Agreement governs how Connect & Grow processes personal data on your behalf. By using our services, you agree to these terms.

1. Definitions

  • "Controller": You, the customer organization using our Platform
  • "Processor": Connect & Grow, processing data on your behalf
  • "Personal Data": Contact information, phone numbers, names, and other data you upload
  • "Processing": Any operation performed on data (collection, storage, transmission, deletion)
  • "Sub-processor": Third-party services we use (e.g., Twilio, cloud hosting)
  • "Data Subject": Individuals whose data is processed (your contacts)

2. Scope and Purpose

This DPA applies to all personal data processed by Connect & Grow on your behalf through our Platform, including:

  • Contact lists and customer databases
  • Phone numbers and names
  • Campaign messages and scripts
  • Call recordings and message logs
  • Delivery and response data

3. Roles and Responsibilities

3.1 Your Responsibilities (as Controller)

You are responsible for:

  • Ensuring you have legal basis to process personal data
  • Obtaining necessary consents from data subjects
  • Providing privacy notices to data subjects
  • Ensuring data accuracy and relevance
  • Complying with applicable data protection laws
  • Responding to data subject requests (access, deletion, etc.)
  • Maintaining records of processing activities

3.2 Our Responsibilities (as Processor)

We will:

  • Process data only according to your documented instructions
  • Implement appropriate security measures
  • Maintain confidentiality of personal data
  • Assist with data subject requests when feasible
  • Notify you of data breaches within 72 hours
  • Delete or return data upon termination
  • Provide information necessary for compliance audits

4. Data Processing Instructions

We will process personal data only for the following purposes:

  • Sending voice calls and WhatsApp messages as instructed by you
  • Storing contact lists and campaign data
  • Generating analytics and delivery reports
  • Providing customer support
  • Maintaining platform security and functionality
Important: We will not process data for any other purpose without your prior written consent.

5. Security Measures

We implement technical and organizational measures including:

5.1 Technical Measures

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS/HTTPS)
  • Encryption of data at rest
  • Access controls and authentication (2FA available)
  • Regular security updates and patches
  • Intrusion detection and prevention systems
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing

5.2 Organizational Measures

  • Employee confidentiality agreements
  • Security awareness training
  • Access limited to authorized personnel only
  • Incident response procedures
  • Data breach notification protocols

6. Sub-processors

We use the following sub-processors to provide our services:

Sub-processor Purpose Location
Twilio Inc. Voice and messaging services USA (GDPR compliant)
Cloud Hosting Provider Data storage and platform hosting India/USA
Payment Gateway Payment processing India

We will notify you of any changes to sub-processors with 30 days notice. You may object to new sub-processors within this period.

7. Data Subject Rights

We will assist you in responding to data subject requests for:

  • Access: Providing copies of personal data
  • Rectification: Correcting inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Deleting data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction: Limiting processing of data
  • Portability: Exporting data in machine-readable format
  • Objection: Stopping certain types of processing

8. Data Breach Notification

In case of a personal data breach, we will:

  • Notify you within 72 hours of becoming aware
  • Provide details of the breach (nature, affected data, estimated impact)
  • Describe measures taken to address the breach
  • Recommend actions you should take
  • Assist in notifying affected data subjects if required

9. Data Transfers

If data is transferred outside India, we ensure:

  • Adequate level of protection in destination country
  • Standard contractual clauses are in place
  • Compliance with applicable data transfer regulations
  • Your explicit consent when required

10. Data Retention and Deletion

10.1 Retention Period

We retain personal data for:

  • Duration of your active subscription
  • 90 days after account termination (configurable)
  • Longer periods if required by law

10.2 Deletion

Upon termination or your request, we will:

  • Delete all personal data within 30 days
  • Provide certification of deletion upon request
  • Ensure sub-processors also delete the data
  • Retain only what is legally required

11. Audits and Compliance

You have the right to:

  • Request information about our processing activities
  • Conduct audits (with reasonable notice)
  • Review our security certifications
  • Inspect our data processing facilities (by appointment)

12. Liability and Indemnification

Each party is liable for damages caused by:

  • Failure to comply with data protection obligations
  • Processing data outside the scope of instructions
  • Inadequate security measures

13. Term and Termination

This DPA:

  • Begins when you start using our services
  • Continues for the duration of your subscription
  • Survives termination for obligations related to data deletion

14. Governing Law

This DPA is governed by:

  • Information Technology Act, 2000 (India)
  • Personal Data Protection Bill (India)
  • GDPR (for EU data subjects)
  • Applicable state and central laws of India
