Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as a "broadcast"?
One broadcast = one automated voice call OR one WhatsApp message sent to a single recipient. Broadcasts are counted only for successfully dispatched messages.
Can I switch plans later?
Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your Organisation dashboard. Changes take effect immediately.
Do I need my own Twilio account?
No. CalltoConvey provides platform-level communication credits by default. You can optionally plug in your own Twilio or Hooman Labs API keys for greater control and lower per-message costs.
Is there a per-message rate beyond the plan limit?
Yes. Additional broadcasts beyond your monthly limit are billed at ₹0.20 per broadcast for voice and ₹0.15 per WhatsApp message.
How is data isolated between organisations?
CalltoConvey uses strict multi-tenant data partitioning. Every organisation's data, workers, campaigns, and modules are completely invisible to all other tenants.
