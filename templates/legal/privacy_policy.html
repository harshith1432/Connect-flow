Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 24, 2026

Your Privacy Matters: This Privacy Policy explains how Connect & Grow collects, uses, and protects your personal information. We are committed to transparency and data protection.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email, phone number, organization details
  • Billing Information: Payment details, billing address (processed securely through payment gateways)
  • Profile Information: Logo, industry, organization type, website
  • Contact Data: Contact lists you upload for campaigns
  • Communication Content: Scripts, messages, and campaign content

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent on platform
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system
  • Campaign Metrics: Delivery status, response rates, call duration
  • Cookies: Session cookies for authentication and preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Process your transactions and subscriptions
  • Send campaign messages on your behalf
  • Improve and optimize our platform
  • Provide customer support
  • Send important service notifications
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 Third-Party Service Providers

We share data with trusted partners who help us operate:

  • Twilio: For voice and messaging services
  • Payment Processors: For secure payment processing
  • Cloud Hosting: For data storage and platform hosting
  • Analytics Tools: For platform improvement

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes or government requests
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Protect our rights, property, or safety
  • Prevent fraud or security threats

3.3 Business Transfers

In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the new entity.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encryption of sensitive data at rest
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Secure data centers with physical security
  • Regular backups and disaster recovery plans
Note: While we implement strong security measures, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as:

  • Your account is active
  • Needed to provide services to you
  • Required by law or for legitimate business purposes
  • Necessary to resolve disputes or enforce agreements

Default retention period: 90 days after account deletion, unless legally required to retain longer.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Correction

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Update your profile and preferences

6.2 Data Portability

You can export your data in standard formats (CSV, JSON) from your account dashboard.

6.3 Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and data by contacting support. Some data may be retained as required by law.

6.4 Marketing Communications

You can opt-out of marketing emails by clicking "unsubscribe" in any marketing email. Service-related communications cannot be opted out.

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies for:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for platform functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: To understand usage patterns
  • Preference Cookies: To remember your settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we discover such data, we will delete it promptly.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via:

  • Email notification
  • Platform notification
  • Updated "Last Modified" date
